# life-social

> Goal: An extremely active social life. Lots of friends, lots of hobbies with them, regular rituals, living close to people I care about, deep community investment.

The risk: Cancelling plans when busy. Letting months drift. Replying to messages is not the same as investing in friendship.

---

## How to use this
- Track social investments as issues: events planned, rituals to establish, friends to reconnect with
- Use `recurring` for rituals that should happen regularly (weekly sport, monthly dinners, etc.)
- If replying to someone is the only social activity tracked in a week — that's not enough
